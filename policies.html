Shop Policies

All items are in perfect or near perfect condition. Any noticeable flaws will be listed in the items' description.

My shipping system is not only fast and affordable, it's green! All items are sent in a biodegradable mailer with no extra packaging. Inside, you'll find your item is wrapped in ribbon made of raffia (biodegradable material). As of September 2023, I am phasing out the traditional thank you note and opting for a thank you message to buyers in app instead.

NO trades or lowball offers. Refunds can be arranged through Depop messages.
